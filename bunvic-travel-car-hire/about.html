About Bunvic Tours

East Africa’s trusted partner for car hire, transfers & safari experiences since 2005.

Brief Overview

The Company was founded by Charles Bundi who, after working for Express Travel Group (a top travel & tour company), decided to start a car hire business because of his extensive experience.

He registered the business as Bunvic Car Hire. “Bunvic” combines the first three letters of his name Bundi and his wife’s name Victoria. The business was registered under number 430730 on December 14th, 2005.

The business deals with car hire, safari bookings, hotel & lodge reservations, excursions, taxis and shuttles. Currently, the business has 2 saloon cars, 3 seven-seater Noah, and 7 nine-seater vans, with 16 drivers and 4 supervisors. Additional vehicles are hired when workloads or guest demand increases.
